
Inca
  - History is told from the perspective of the Spaniards
  - Road system was key to the empire; with storage houses every few miles
  - Tax collectors also keep in check their territories
  - People who were not subordinate to rule were forced to move around the empire
  - Mita System - System of labor
  - Local rules are removed in favor of people who have kinship with the ruler

Aztecs
  - Access to trade routes
  - Didn't worry too much about expanding -- more for sacrifices
  - Keep same people governing (locally, autonomously) as long as they provide tributes
  - Economic system
    - There isn't a "lord"
    - Lack of currency
    - No private enterprise (everything is owned by the state)
